What is Trading?

Trading is the activity of buying and selling financial assets such as stocks, forex, and cryptocurrency. The main goal of trading is to earn profit.

Types of Trading

There are different types of trading such as day trading, swing trading, and long-term investing. Each type has its own strategy and risk level.
